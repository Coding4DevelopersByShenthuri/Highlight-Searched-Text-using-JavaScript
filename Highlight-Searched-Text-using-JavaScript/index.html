Robotics is an interdisciplinary field that integrates computer science and engineering to design, construct, operate, and use robots. These machines are capable of performing a variety of tasks, either autonomously or under human guidance, by interpreting data from sensors and making decisions based on pre-defined algorithms or artificial intelligence. Robotics has a wide range of applications, from manufacturing and healthcare to space exploration and entertainment, continually pushing the boundaries of what machines can achieve in both the physical and digital realms.
